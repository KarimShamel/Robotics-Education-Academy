Impact Quest (IQ)
Engineering the Future.

Dive into the world of snap-together robotics. The IQ program empowers students to design, build, and program sophisticated robots while mastering the fundamentals of teamwork and engineering design.

Master the Mechanics of Innovation

Impact Quest is more than just building robots. It's a comprehensive educational framework that transforms abstract STEM concepts into tangible, hands-on challenges. Students progress from simple mechanical structures to advanced, sensor-driven autonomous machines.
